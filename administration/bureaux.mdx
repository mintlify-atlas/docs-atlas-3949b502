---
title: "Bureaux"
description: "Manage the office units within your organization"
---

A **bureau** is an office unit inside your organization. Trainees (stagiaires) are assigned to a bureau for the duration of their internship. Each bureau belongs to exactly one service (department), so your organizational structure flows from service down to bureau and then to the individual trainee.

<Note>
  You must create at least one service before you can create a bureau. Bureaux cannot exist without a parent service.
</Note>

## Listing bureaux

Navigate to the **Bureaux** section from the sidebar. The page displays a table with two columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Bureau | The name (libellé) of the bureau |
| Service | The service this bureau belongs to |

Each row has edit and delete action buttons.

## Creating a bureau

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the create form">
    On the Bureaux list page, click the **+ Ajouter nouveau Bureau** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the bureau name">
    Fill in the **Libellé du bureau** field with the office unit's name. This field is required and must not exceed 255 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the parent service">
    Choose the service this bureau belongs to from the **Service** dropdown. The dropdown lists all services currently in the system. This field is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Ajouter** to create the bureau. You will be redirected back to the bureaux list with a confirmation message. Click **Annuler** at any time to discard the form and return to the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a bureau

1. On the Bureaux list page, click the edit icon next to the bureau you want to update.
2. The edit form opens pre-filled with the bureau's current **Libellé** and **Service**.
3. Update either field as needed and click **Modifier** to save your changes.
4. Click **Annuler** to discard your changes and return to the list.

## Deleting a bureau

On the Bureaux list page, click the delete icon next to the bureau you want to remove. A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to confirm the deletion. Click **OK** to proceed.

<Note>
  Deleting a bureau is permanent. Make sure no trainees are currently assigned to the bureau before deleting it.
</Note>
