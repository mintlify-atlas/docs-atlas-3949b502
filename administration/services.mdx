---
title: "Services"
description: "Manage the service departments within your organization"
---

A **service** is a top-level department in your organization. Services sit at the top of the organizational hierarchy — bureaux (office units) belong to services, and trainees are then assigned to those bureaux:

```
Service → Bureau → Stagiaire
```

You need at least one service before you can create any bureaux or assign trainees.

<Tip>
  Set up your services first. Because bureaux require a parent service, the recommended setup order is: create services, then create bureaux, then register trainees.
</Tip>

## Listing services

Navigate to the **Services** section from the sidebar. The page displays a paginated table (5 services per page) with the following column:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Libellé | The name of the service/department |

Each row has edit and delete action buttons. Use the pagination controls at the bottom of the table to move between pages.

## Creating a service

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the create form">
    On the Services list page, click the **+ Ajouter nouveau service** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the service name">
    Fill in the **Libellé du service** field with the department name. This field is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Ajouter** to create the service. You will be redirected back to the services list with a confirmation message. Click **Annuler** at any time to discard the form and return to the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a service

1. On the Services list page, click the edit icon next to the service you want to update.
2. The edit form opens pre-filled with the service's current **Libellé**.
3. Update the name and click **Modifier** to save your changes.
4. Click **Annuler** to discard your changes and return to the list.

## Deleting a service

On the Services list page, click the delete icon next to the service you want to remove. A confirmation dialog will appear. Click **OK** to confirm the deletion.

<Note>
  Deleting a service is permanent. Before removing a service, ensure that all bureaux belonging to it have been reassigned or deleted, as bureaux require a valid parent service.
</Note>
