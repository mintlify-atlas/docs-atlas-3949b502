## Registering an account

Go to `/register` to create a new account. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Name | Your display name |
| Email | Your email address (used to log in) |
| Password | A secure password |
| Confirm Password | Re-enter your password to confirm |

Click **Register** to submit. You will be taken directly to your dashboard upon successful registration.

## Logging in

Go to `/login` and enter your registered email address and password. Click **Log in** to access your dashboard.

If you have forgotten your password, click the **Forgot your password?** link on the login page. Enter your email address and a password reset link will be sent to your inbox.

<Note>
  All dashboard pages — including bureaux, services, etablissements, trainees, and attestations — require you to be logged in. You will be redirected to the login page if you try to access them while signed out.
</Note>

## Profile management

Navigate to `/profile` (or click your profile link in the navigation) to manage your account details. The profile page has three sections:

### Update profile information

Update your **Name** and **Email** fields and click **Save** to apply the changes.

### Update password

To change your password:

1. Enter your **Current Password**.
2. Enter a **New Password**.
3. Re-enter the new password in the **Confirm Password** field.
4. Click **Save**.

### Resetting a forgotten password

If you are already logged out and cannot remember your password, use the **Forgot your password?** link on the login page instead.

## Deleting your account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be irreversibly removed. Download or export any information you need before proceeding.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

1. Go to `/profile`.
2. Scroll to the **Delete Account** section at the bottom of the page.
3. Click the **Delete Account** button.
4. A confirmation dialog will appear. Enter your current password to confirm.
5. Click **Delete Account** in the dialog to permanently delete your account.

You will be signed out and redirected to the home page immediately after deletion.
