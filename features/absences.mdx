The absences module lets you log and track periods when a trainee was absent. Each absence record is linked to a specific trainee and captures the start date, end date, and a written justification.

## What an absence record contains

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Stagiaire | The trainee this absence is attributed to |
| Date de début | The first day of the absence |
| Date de fin | The last day of the absence (can be the same day as the start date) |
| Justification | A text description of the reason for the absence (max 250 characters) |

## Listing absences

Navigate to **Absences** from the sidebar, or go directly to `/absences/index`. The page shows a table of all recorded absences with the linked trainee's name, the absence dates, and the justification.

You can also use the search bar to filter the list — see [Filtering absences](#filtering-absences) below.

## Recording an absence

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the creation form">
    Click the **Ajouter une absence** button on the absences listing page, or navigate to `/absences/create`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the trainee">
    Choose the relevant trainee from the **Stagiaire** dropdown. Only existing registered trainees appear in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the absence dates">
    Enter the **Date de début** (start date) and **Date de fin** (end date). The end date can be the same as the start date for a single-day absence. The end date cannot be before the start date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the justification">
    Write a brief explanation in the **Justification** field (required, max 250 characters).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Enregistrer**. You will be redirected to the absences list with a success message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Form fields

<ParamField body="stagiaire_id" type="integer" required>
  The ID of the trainee. Selected from the dropdown on the form. Must reference an existing stagiaire.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="date_debut" type="date" required>
  First day of the absence. Must be a valid date.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="date_fin" type="date" required>
  Last day of the absence. Must be a valid date and must be on or after `date_debut`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="justification" type="string" required>
  Reason for the absence. Plain text, maximum 250 characters.
</ParamField>

## Editing an absence

1. From the absences list, click the **Edit** button on the row you want to update. This opens `/absences/edit/{id}`.
2. Adjust the trainee selection, dates, or justification as needed.
3. Click **Enregistrer** to save. You will be redirected to the absences list with a confirmation message.

## Deleting an absence

Click the **Delete** button on the row you want to remove and confirm the action. The record is deleted immediately and you are returned to the absences list.

## Filtering absences

The absences list page includes a search bar that filters records by keyword. The search matches across:

- Trainee's nom (family name)
- Trainee's prénom (given name)
- Trainee's email
- Trainee's tél (phone)
- Date de début
- Date de fin
- Justification text

Type a keyword and submit to narrow the list. Clear the field and resubmit to return to the full list.
