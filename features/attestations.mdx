An attestation is the official completion certificate issued to a trainee at the end of their internship. It links a trainee to their stage and records which bureau and service oversaw the placement, along with the delivery status of the physical certificate.

<Note>
  Attestation records are created directly in the database (for example via seeders or database tooling). The application provides views for managing, updating status, generating PDFs, and deleting existing attestation records.
</Note>

## What an attestation record contains

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Stagiaire | The trainee the certificate belongs to |
| Stage | The internship assignment the certificate covers |
| Bureau | The office that managed the trainee's placement |
| Service | The specific department or service within the bureau |
| Établissement | The trainee's educational institution (shown on the list, sourced from the trainee's profile) |
| Statut | Delivery status: **remis** (delivered) or **non remis** (not yet delivered) |
| Date de prise | The date the certificate was handed to the trainee (set automatically when the status is changed to **remis**) |

## Listing attestations

Navigate to **Attestations** from the sidebar, or go directly to `/Attestations`. The page shows a table of all attestations enriched with related data — trainee name, stage title, bureau, service, and établissement — so you have a complete picture without drilling into individual records.

From this page you can update the delivery status, generate a PDF certificate, or delete a record.

## Updating delivery status

Each attestation has a status selector that lets you mark a certificate as delivered or not yet delivered.

| Status | Meaning | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `remis` | Certificate has been physically handed to the trainee | `date_prise` is automatically set to the current date and time |
| `nonremis` | Certificate has not yet been delivered | `date_prise` is not updated |

To change the status:

1. On the attestations list page, locate the attestation row you want to update.
2. Change the status selector to **remis** or **non remis**.
3. Submit the form. The page reloads with a success message confirming the update.

<Note>
  When you set the status to **remis**, the system records the current timestamp as the `date_prise` automatically. You do not need to enter the date manually.
</Note>

## Generating a PDF certificate

<Tip>
  You can generate the PDF directly from the attestations list page without opening a separate form — just click the **Générer PDF** button on the relevant row.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the attestation">
    On the attestations list page (`/Attestations`), find the row for the trainee you want to issue a certificate for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Générer PDF">
    Click the **Générer PDF** button on that row. The browser will navigate to `/generate-pdf/{id}`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or print the PDF">
    The PDF is streamed directly to your browser as `attestation.pdf`. Depending on your browser settings, it will either open inline for printing or prompt you to save the file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The generated PDF is built from the attestation data and includes the trainee's full details, their stage, bureau, service, and établissement.

## Deleting an attestation

Click the **Delete** button on the attestation row you want to remove and confirm the action. The record is deleted immediately and you are returned to the attestations list with a success message.

<Warning>
  Deleting an attestation is permanent. If you need to re-issue a certificate, you will need to recreate the attestation record first.
</Warning>
