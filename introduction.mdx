Manitern Pro is a web-based internship management system built for organizations that host trainees (stagiaires). It gives your team a centralized workspace to manage every stage of an internship lifecycle: from onboarding trainees and assigning them to bureaux, to recording absences and issuing official attestation PDFs.

## What you can do with Manitern Pro

Manitern Pro covers every operational need of an internship program:

- **Trainees (Stagiaires)** — Register trainees with full profile data including CIN, institution, stage assignment, and bureau. Upload and manage CV files.
- **Internships (Stages)** — Create and track internship subjects with start/end dates and status lifecycle.
- **Absences** — Record and filter trainee absences with justification and date ranges.
- **Attestations** — Generate and export signed PDF attestation documents. Track delivery status (remis / non remis).
- **Institutions (Etablissements)** — Maintain a directory of partner institutions and link trainees to them.
- **Services & Bureaux** — Organize your org structure into services and bureaux, then assign trainees accordingly.
- **Dashboard** — Get an at-a-glance overview of all activity with live statistics and charts.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the application">
    Clone the repository, install PHP and JavaScript dependencies, and configure your `.env` file. See [Installation](/getting-started/installation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your environment">
    Set up your database connection and run migrations to create all required tables. See [Configuration](/getting-started/configuration).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your admin account">
    Register the first user account through the `/register` route. This account will have full access to all features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your organization structure">
    Start by creating your [Services](/administration/services), then [Bureaux](/administration/bureaux), and [Etablissements](/administration/etablissements) before adding trainees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your first trainee">
    Navigate to **Stagiaires → Add Stagiaire**, fill in the trainee's details, and upload their CV. The trainee is now tracked in the system.
  </Step>
</Steps>
